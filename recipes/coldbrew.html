Cold Brew Coffee

Description

Cold brew coffee is by no means created by the McCarty family, but this recipe is used almost daily by us. Cold brew has a higher caffiene content due to the nature of the longer brewing duration.

Naturally, the McCarty family adopted this brewing process into our lives.

Ingredients

  • Bag of coffee
  • Water
  • Sealable container
  • Cooler/Refrigerator
  • Cheese cloth

Steps

  1. Measure two cups of coffee into your sealable container
  2. Stir in ten cups of filtered water
  3. Mix the coffee and water thoroughly
  4. Seal container and place in cooler/refrigerator
  5. Let sit for 12-24 hours
  6. Once brewed strain coffee through cheese cloth into new container
  7. Enjoy!
