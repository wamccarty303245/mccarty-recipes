Apple Cinnamon Oats

Description

Cinnamon oats are a perfect snack that can be prepared overnight for a quick breakfast come morning time.

Ingredients

  • Old fashioned oats
  • Cinnamon
  • Salt
  • Half of a medium apple (green)
  • Unsweet vanilla almond milk
  • Sugar free maple syrup
  • Greek yogurt

Steps

  1. Mix together 2/3 cup of old fashioned oats
  2. 1 tsp cinnamon
  3. A pinch of Salt
  4. Half of a green apple, peeled and diced
  5. 3/4 cup of unsweet almond milk
  6. 3 tbsp sugar free maple syrup
  7. 1/2 cup greek yogurt
  8. Seal container, let sit in refrigerator overnight
  9. Enjoy!
